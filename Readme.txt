What are Collections?
- It is a set of classes and interfaces to implement reusable data structures
- Basically it is a group of classes and interfaces that provide us with an ability to manipulate the data.
- Used for stroing and manipulating a group of objects. You can insert, search, sort, delete the data.
- It reduces a programmers effort.
- It increases the speed of performance.
- Efficient memery management.

What are Wrapper Classes:
It wraps your primitive datatype into an object. Provide you with the objects of same datatype.
